About us

This platform allows you to report and track issues on the road to help improve community safety. You can report issues, upload pictures, and discuss with other community members.
The Road Issue Reporter platform is dedicated to improving community safety by allowing users to report and track road issues. By providing detailed reports and facilitating discussions, we aim to help local authorities address problems more efficiently.
